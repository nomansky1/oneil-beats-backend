## 📊 Day-1 paid ad (optional, $20-50 budget cap)

### Google Ads "App install" campaign
- **Targeting**: Spanish-speaking US (CA, TX, FL, NY) + Mexico + Puerto Rico
- **Audience interests**: Music production, Beats, Reggaeton, Trap, Hip-hop
- **Bid**: tCPI (target cost per install) — start at $1.50, raise to $2 if volume is low
- **Budget cap**: $20/day for first 3 days, scale based on installs/day
- **Creative**: 30s vertical video of app demo + "Download OB Beats — your beats, your pocket"

### Meta Ads
- **Targeting**: Same audience + lookalikes from your IG followers
- **Format**: Reels-style mobile-first vertical
- **Budget cap**: $20/day, mirror Google
- **Goal**: App install events (track via the existing Meta SDK in the app)

## 📈 What to monitor (Day 1-7)

| Metric | Where | Target Day 1 | Target Day 7 |
|---|---|---|---|
| App Store impressions | ASC → App Analytics | 500+ | 2k+ |
| Downloads | ASC | 30+ | 200+ |
| First-tap → first-play rate | GA4 events `first_open` → `play` | 60%+ | 70%+ |
| First-tap → first-license-click | GA4 `click_lease/premium/stems` | 5%+ | 10%+ |
| Email opt-ins from app | Supabase `email_subscribers` | 10+ | 50+ |
| App Store rating (week 1) | ASC → Reviews | n/a | 4.5+ avg, 10+ ratings |

## 🔁 Day 7 retro — assess + adjust

After 7 days look at:
- Which channel drove most installs? Double down there.
- Which beats got most plays? Promote those harder.
- Which license tier converted best? Adjust pricing or hierarchy.
- Did email open rates exceed 30%? If yes, increase send cadence.
