Publishes to your own YouTube channel

CoverLoop uses YouTube API Services. You sign in with your own Google account and grant permission, and CoverLoop uploads the videos you create to your channel and shows you your analytics. Your audio and your login stay on your computer. You can revoke access anytime at Google account permissions. CoverLoop is not affiliated with or endorsed by YouTube or Google.
