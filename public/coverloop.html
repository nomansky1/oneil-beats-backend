Turn your beats into YouTube videos.

CoverLoop renders channel-quality type-beat and lyric videos right on your PC — AI cover art, reactive visualizers, beat-synced cut-scenes, and high-CTR thumbnails — then publishes them to your own YouTube channel.

What it does

Drop a beat. Get a finished, upload-ready video.

🎨 AI artwork & cut-scenes

Auto-generates album-style cover art and a playlist of AI scenes that cross-dissolve on the beat across your song.

📊 Reactive visualizers

Audio-reactive bars and effects drawn from your beat's own frequencies, with color grades and motion.

🎤 Lyric videos

Add lyrics with styled, beat-timed text — outline, glow, karaoke and more.

🖼️ One-click thumbnails

Generate a click-worthy AI thumbnail with bold text baked on, in a single click.

🗂️ Batch render

Drop a whole folder of beats and render a finished video for every one, unattended.

📅 Scheduled uploads

Queue your videos and let YouTube publish them on a schedule — consistent posting, hands-free.

Publishes to your own YouTube channel

CoverLoop uses YouTube API Services. You sign in with your own Google account and grant permission, and CoverLoop uploads the videos you create to your channel and shows you your analytics. Your audio and login stay on your computer. You can revoke access anytime at Google account permissions. CoverLoop is not affiliated with or endorsed by YouTube or Google.
