FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying rap beats online.

What types of rap beats do you sell?
We sell Trap beats, Drill instrumentals, Boom Bap, Lo-Fi, R&B, Afrobeats, and more. All beats are available in multiple license tiers from $29.99.
Can I use these beats on YouTube and Spotify?
Yes. Lease licenses cover up to 100K streams. Premium Lease and above cover unlimited streaming on YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, and all other platforms.
How do I get my beats after purchase?
Your download links are sent instantly to your email after payment via Stripe. No waiting, no delays — you can start recording within minutes.
Do I need to credit the producer?
For Lease and Premium Lease licenses, yes — include "Prod. by O'Neil" in your song title or description. Exclusive license holders do not need to credit the producer.
What is the difference between Lease and Exclusive?
A Lease is non-exclusive — meaning other artists can also buy the same beat. An Exclusive means you own the beat outright, it gets removed from the store, and no one else can purchase it.
Can I use leased beats for commercial mixtapes?
Yes, Lease and above cover commercial use including mixtapes, EPs, and albums up to the stream/download limits in your license agreement.
What if I lose my download link?
Use the "My Orders" section below — enter your email and your order history will appear with fresh download links.
