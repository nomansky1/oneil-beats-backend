Type Beat Video Maker: Turn Your Beats Into YouTube Videos

If you sell beats, the video is the marketing. A clean type beat video — cover art, a reactive visualizer, and a thumbnail that gets clicks — is what turns a beat into views, leads and sales on YouTube. Here's how to make one fast, and how CoverLoop does the whole thing for you.

How to make a type beat video for YouTube (4 steps)

1. Start with your beat

Drop in your MP3 or WAV. A good type beat video keeps the audio front-and-center, so make sure it's mixed and tagged. CoverLoop reads the BPM, key, genre and mood automatically and uses them to drive the whole look.

2. Make the cover art

The cover sets the vibe. Generate AI artwork from a prompt (free, no account needed) or upload your own. For extra polish, animate it into a moving cover or a playlist of AI cut-scenes that change on the beat.

3. Add the visualizer & look

An audio-reactive bar visualizer drawn from your beat's own frequencies is the single biggest "looks professional" upgrade. Add a color grade, a background, and a styled title, and preview it live before you render.

4. Thumbnail, SEO & publish

Your thumbnail drives more clicks than anything else — bold, readable text over strong art. Then write a title, tags and description that match what's trending in your niche, render the video, and upload it to your channel.

What makes a type beat video get views

Three things move the needle: a thumbnail people can't help clicking, SEO (the right "[artist] type beat" title and tags so the algorithm shows it to the right listeners), and consistency — posting regularly so your channel compounds. The fastest-growing beat channels batch their catalog into videos and post on a schedule.
