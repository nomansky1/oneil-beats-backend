CoverLoop — Privacy Policy

O'Neil Beats · Last Updated: June 15, 2026

The short version: CoverLoop runs on your computer. Your beats, songs, artwork, and rendered videos stay on your device. We do not operate a server that collects your content, and we do not sell your data or use it for advertising. If you connect YouTube, your login is stored only on your device and is used solely to upload your videos to your channel and read your analytics.

This Privacy Policy explains how CoverLoop (the "App") handles your information. CoverLoop is a desktop application published by O'Neil Beats, operated by the producer professionally known as O'Neil ("we", "us", "our"). You can reach us at produceroneil@gmail.com.

What CoverLoop stores — and where

All of the following is stored locally in the App's user-data folder on your device, not on our servers:

  • Your media: the audio you import and the artwork, thumbnails, and videos you generate.
  • Settings: your preferences and any API key you choose to enter (e.g. a Gemini or cloud-video key).
  • Library & analytics history: records of videos you rendered/published and the YouTube performance metrics you choose to refresh.
  • Diagnostics log: a local log of render/upload events used to show you in-app tips. This never leaves your device.

You can delete all of it by removing the App's user-data folder or uninstalling.

Google / YouTube data

If you connect your YouTube channel:

  • CoverLoop uses YouTube API Services and Google OAuth. Your access tokens are stored locally on your device and are used only to (a) upload videos you create to your own channel and (b) read your own channel's details and analytics for the in-app insights.
  • We do not transmit your Google tokens or YouTube data to our servers, and we do not share them with third parties.
  • You can revoke CoverLoop's access at any time at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.
Google API Services Limited Use disclosure. CoverLoop's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We use Google user data only to provide and improve the App's user-facing features, and we do not use it for advertising or sell it.

Third-party AI providers (only if you choose them)

  • If you select an online art/SEO/video provider (e.g. Google Gemini, Pollinations, fal.ai, Runway, Replicate), the prompt text — and, for some features, an image — is sent to that provider to generate the result. That processing is governed by the provider's own privacy policy, under the API key you supply.
  • If you use on-device providers (local Stable Diffusion / Stable Video Diffusion, or local models via Ollama), nothing is sent off your device.

What we don't collect

We do not run analytics/telemetry that reports your usage back to us, we do not track you across apps or sites, and we do not sell personal information.

Payments

If CoverLoop later offers a paid subscription, payment will be handled by a third-party processor (e.g. Stripe or Paddle). We would receive limited billing confirmation (e.g. that a subscription is active); your full card details are handled by the processor, not by us.

Data retention & your choices

  • Local data persists until you delete it.
  • Disconnecting Google removes stored tokens locally.
  • For requests under GDPR/CCPA or similar laws, contact us at the email above.

Children

CoverLoop is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this Policy; material changes will be noted in-app or at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
