Summary: The OB app works in guest mode — no login or password is required. The only personal data we may hold is an email address you voluntarily submitted (for free-beat downloads, purchase receipts, or marketing). You can request deletion of that data at any time using the form below or by emailing us directly.

1. What Data We Can Delete

  • Your email address and any marketing/referral records tied to it.
  • Free-beat download leads linked to your email.
  • Customer event logs (plays, favorites, cart events) linked to your device or email.
  • Push-notification token, if you opted in.

2. What We Cannot Delete

  • Purchase / transaction records: If you bought a license, we're legally required to retain the receipt and license record for tax and copyright-enforcement purposes. Payment details themselves are held by Stripe — we never see your card number.
  • Licenses you've already downloaded: Any beats you've already purchased remain yours to use under the license terms, even after your account data is deleted.

3. How to Request Deletion

Send an email to produceroneil@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from the email address you want deleted.

Email Deletion Request

4. Timeline

We process deletion requests within 7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email once your data has been removed. If we cannot fully delete certain records (see section 2), we'll tell you what we're required to keep and for how long.

5. Questions

Email produceroneil@gmail.com with any questions about this process or your data.
